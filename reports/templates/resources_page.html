Refugee resettlement data is an entry point into new stories, but it can only provide so much information. It can't illustrate important nuances like religious minorities, births and deaths, or previously resettled communities, for example.

This is where reporting comes in. Contacting community leaders and local refugee resettlement agencies is an important first step in connecting to refugee communities and learning about stories behind the numbers. For more information about best practices in covering refugee populations, read our strategy study below.
