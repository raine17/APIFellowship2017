The Refugee Reporting Resource is a tool to help newsrooms provide more complete and multi-dimensional coverage of local refugee communities. To accomplish this, the American Press Institute worked with summer fellow Emily Case to develop a guide that includes a searchable refugee resettlement database and resources about covering local refugee communities.

If you have any questions or comments, contact American Press Institute 2017 Summer Fellow Emily Case.
